Konference - instant webchat in browser

Design modern and simple logo that shows what the company is about talking and communication, along with a website with all needed info for customers, but the most important was the ease of creating chatrooms and sharing them with friend or colleagues so they can join.

Logo is made of four chat bubbles that create K in negative space. Creating a chatroom is seen first on website and is as easy as typing desired name and pressing enter. Same funcionality is accesible in top menu bar after scrolling down. Each aspect of the product is shortly described on separate panels with icons designed to match logo colours.
